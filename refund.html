Refund and Cancellation Policy

Refund Policy

At Reddtribe HD, we strive to provide you with the best IPTV experience possible. We understand that circumstances may arise where you need to request a refund. This policy outlines the conditions and process for refund requests.

3-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 3-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you are not completely satisfied with our service within the first 3 days of your subscription, you may request a full refund.

Eligibility For Refund

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:
- The refund request must be made within 3 days of the initial subscription purchase.
- The subscription must be in its initial term, and not a renewal or extension.
- The account requesting the refund must be in good standing, with no violations of our Terms of Service.

How To Request A Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:
- Contact our support team at refund@reddtribehd.com with your refund request.
- Provide your account details, including the email address associated with your Reddtribe HD account.
- Clearly state the reason for your refund request.
- Allow up to 7 business days for your refund request to be processed.

Exceptions To The Refund Policy

Refunds may not be granted in the following cases:
- Violations of Terms: If your account is found to be in violation of our Terms of Service, you will not be eligible for a refund.
- Renewals or Extensions: Refunds are only applicable to new subscriptions and not for subscription renewals or extensions.
- Technical Issues: If you encounter technical issues, we encourage you to reach out to our support team for assistance before requesting a refund.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy, please feel free to contact our support team. We're here to assist you and ensure you have a positive experience with Reddtribe HD.
