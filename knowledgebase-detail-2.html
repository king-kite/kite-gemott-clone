Setting up IPTV on Android Using IPTV Smarters App

IPTV Smarters is an Android application based on Xtream Codes that allows your valued customers to enjoy Live TV, VOD, Series, and TV Catchup on their Android devices such as mobile phones, Android boxes, Fire TV Stick, and more

Features of IPTV Smarters:

  • Live, Movies, Series & TV Catchup Streaming
  • EPG Support
  • Integrated Parental Control for each category
  • Powerful built-in player
  • Support for Playing with External Players
  • Attractive & User-Friendly Layout
  • Dynamic Language Switching Support

Please note that you will need to input your Username, Password and Server URL provided by your IPTV service provider.

Step 1: Download and Install Downloader App
- Dismiss prompts until you see the URL text box on the right side. Make sure the "Home" option in the left menu is selected.
- Enter the following path for SMATERS PRO APP and click "GO".

Step 2: Installing IPTV Smarters.
- Wait for a minute or two until the player is downloaded onto your device.
- Click "Install" when the download is complete.
- Wait for the installation to finish.
- Once installed, you'll receive an "App installed" notification.

Step 3: Cleaning Up.
You can choose to click "OPEN" and start using the IBO Player or select "DONE" to delete the APK file and save storage space on your device.

Step 4: Adding IPTV Channels.
- Open the app and select "LOGIN WITH XTREAM CODES API".
- In the first box, choose any name you prefer. In the second and third boxes, enter your M3U line's "Username" and "Password", as well as the server URL provided via email or found in your client area under services.
- Click "ADD USER" to proceed.

Step 5: Accessing Live TV Channels.
- Click on the "Live TV" icon.
- Select the channel group you want to watch.

Step 6: Watching a Channel.
Click on the channel name and then double-click on the small screen to go full screen.
You're now all set up with IPTV Smarters on your Android device! Enjoy your content.
